Compliance & Approvals
Sponsorship Compliance Automation
SharePoint Online · PowerApps · Power Automate · PDF generation
Before

Email-based submissions, inconsistent data, and manual PDF creation made the sponsorship process slow and hard to audit.

Solution

Built a structured SharePoint list and PowerApps form with dynamic fields, automatic approver routing, encoded edit links, and HTML-to-PDF generation. Approvers receive a button that opens the specific request directly in Edit mode.

Large Lists
SharePoint Calendar Threshold Optimization
SharePoint Online · List indexing
Challenge

A calendar with thousands of events was hitting the 5,000-item list view threshold, causing errors and slow loads.

Solution

Indexed key date columns and designed a view that focuses on an 8-month history and 2-month future window, keeping the view under the threshold while preserving visibility.

Impact

Views load quickly and reliably, and site owners understand how to keep them that way as the calendar grows.

Indexing Performance
External Access
QR Code Access for Non-AD Contractors
SharePoint Online · External sharing · QR
Challenge

Contractors without Entra accounts needed quick access to specific equipment documents on the shop floor, without broad access to the tenant.

Solution

Configured a SharePoint site to safely host documentation with tightly scoped share links, and generated QR codes pointing to specific files. Validated the user experience from a non-tenant perspective.

Impact

Contractors scan a code, view the correct document, and never see anything else in the environment.

How I Work
A lightweight, collaborative approach that respects IT constraints while giving business teams what they need.
  • Step 1
    Discovery & quick audit
    We review your current process (forms, email chains, spreadsheets) and clarify the real business rules and exceptions that matter.
  • Step 2
    Solution design
    I propose a SharePoint/Power Platform architecture: lists, forms, flows, and any PDFs or integrations. You get a short written outline before we build.
  • Step 3
    Build & iterate
    I implement the solution in your tenant, with checkpoints where your key users can review and give feedback. Adjustments are built into the process.
  • Step 4
    Launch & handoff
    We go live with clear documentation for admins and end users, and optionally a support window for tweaks after real-world use.
